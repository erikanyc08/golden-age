Expert Health & Nursing Care

Our expert health professionals can assist with scheduling appointments and arranging transportation for physical and occupational therapy.

We also have an in-house Podiatrist that provide podiatry services as well as assist to obtain orthopedic shoes and durable medical equipment, as per doctor’s order.

Activities & Parties

We design our daily programs in the way to meet the needs and interests of each of our client.
Our activities include: music, special occasion parties, bingo, mahjong, dominoes, Tai Chi, arts & crafts, card & board games, educational programs with guest speakers, shopping trips etc.
We also celebrate major traditional events with special guests from different communities.

Transportation Services

We provide complimentary transportation To and From the facility for all our clients.
Our professional and precaution bus drivers greet each client and provide assistance if needed upon arrival and departure.

Transportation to medical appointments, shopping trips or other social outings is provided with no extra charge.

Balanced Nutrition

Maintaining a well-balanced diet is extremely important for the health of our clients, therefore we carefully think through our menu.
Under the direction of our registered dietician, we serve high-quality nutritious meals based on the traditions of our multicultural clients.
Coffee and tea are always available in between meals.

We also provide dietary monitoring and counseling.

Social Worker Services

Our licensed high-professional social workers offer individual and family counseling on social, financial, legal, educational needs, including assistance with all community services such as food stamps, housing, homecare services; we also provide family outreach.
Our Social workers address the clients’ needs and navigate the challenges of living in the community.

Alzheimer & Dementia Care

We provide professional services to the clients who suffer from different cognitive diagnoses including Alzheimer’s, Dementia due Parkinson’s, other types of Dementia.
Our facility is reliably secured by installed wander guard management systems, which provides a secure environment for our members and allow them to walk freely and securely which gives families peace of mind.
