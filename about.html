About our Facility

For more than 15 years, we provide professional support to families who are taking care of elderly and adults with functional disabilities, memory impairment, and may require continuous assistance or supervision. We offer an ideal alternative to caregivers by providing a daytime care environment, which prevents living in isolation or in nursing homes.
Our daily programs encourage your loved ones to be happy, remain independent and enjoy high quality of life with expert nursing care, nutritious meals, enriching activities, and social services in a safe and loving atmosphere.
From the bottom of our hearts, we offer the highest quality of care, which is helping our clients to achieve healthier and happier way of life.
We are proud to be a vibrant, culturally diverse community where everyone promotes tolerance and respect. Our multilingual, professional team brings deep empathy and sensitivity to the programs and care we provide.
We offer our services to a variety of cultural groups that include Korean, Chinese, Vietnamese, Filipino, Spanish, American, and Russian. We offer culturally diverse meals, activities, and holiday celebrations.
Our services are 100% covered by Medicaid. Our Social Workers can help you to determine your eligibility for the programs, and will help you to apply for Medicaid if you need to. Private pay is also accepted in our center.

Covered by Medicaid

Our Social Workers can help you to determine your eligibility for medical waiver programs, and will help you to apply for Medicaid if you needed.

Expert Care

Our registered nurses are on duty full time providing skilled, compassionate medical care.

Safety is a Priority

Our facility is reliably secured by Wander Management Alert Systems, which gives Alzheimer’s, Dementia and other “at-risk” members the ability to move freely about facility while receiving the protection they and their families need.

Free Transportation

We provide complimentary transportation To and From the facility, as well as transfer to medical appointments, shopping trips or other social outings.

Multilingual Staff

We are proud of our multicultural and diverse competency that deeply integrated to the programs and care we provide. We speak different languages, which include but not limited to: English, Korean, Chinese, Vietnamese, Filipino, Spanish and Russian.

Nutritious Meals & Snacks

Under the direction of our registered dietician, we serve well-balanced meals based on the traditions of our multicultural clients. Coffee and tea are always available in between meals.

Memory Care

We provide professional services to the clients with different cognitive diagnoses including Alzheimer’s, Dementia due to Parkinson’s, other types of Dementia.

Engaging Activities

Our considerate daily program encourages our clients. We provide an array of activities to enhance our clients overall well-being. We celebrate major Traditional Events with special guests from the Communities. We offer culturally diverse meals, activities, and holiday celebrations.
