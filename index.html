Our Mission

For more than 15 years Golden Age Adult Medical Day Care Center has been dedicated to provide the highest quality of care to seniors or disabled adults while caring for them with love, compassion and respect.
Through the enriching activities, opportunities for socializing, peer support, expert health and nursing care we promote members' ability remain independent and enjoy high quality of life.
Every single day we strive for excellence and total satisfaction of our clients and peace of mind of their families and loved ones.
